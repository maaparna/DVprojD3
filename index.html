The IMDB movies and ratings

This page gives you a view of the IMDB ratings of the movies from the year 2006 to 2016.

The chart on the left consolidates the rating by Genre for the selected year.

When a genre is selected, a new chart below lists the movies and their ratings.

In the bottom chart, when a movie is selected, a scorecard with further details about the movie will be displayed.

Also, note that the same process would apply to any year selected in the dropdown list provided below the title.

Please select a genre on the left chart and scroll down to see the list of movies

In the bottom chart, please select a movie to see further details in a scorecard.
